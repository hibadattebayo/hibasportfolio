More about me

This is me!

I’m a UI UX designer that understands the basics of front-end development.


The basics being html and css (and I am trying to understand javascript which is a little mind blowing for me at the moment). I am also continuously learning about new frameworks and programs. As of now, I can work with programs like photoshop, illustrator, premiere pro, Figma, Axure, Github desktop etc.


I love to design for the web and I love to take on new challenges that can wow both the people using my stuff and the ones that I am making it for.


How I work

When I design, I make sure to get straight who i do it for first. I need to know what the possible problem is I have to solve with my design. Then I go over the needs of the client and try to match them to the goals they want to achieve with the design. After having a, what I like to call: "therapy-session" with the client, I brainstorm and look for inspiration.

I look at other designs, magazines competitors etc. and eventually come up with one or most of the time, more ideas. After this I start designing the first prototypes. I like to continuously involve my clients with my work so that I do what they want while also making something user friendly. And after some iterations and talks, it is there. TADAAA ur design is done!
